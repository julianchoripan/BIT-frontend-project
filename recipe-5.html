Rigatoni with Panko

Rigatoni with Panko is a delightful Italian dish known for its unique texture and savory flavor. This comforting meal features al dente rigatoni pasta baked with a golden, crispy panko breadcrumb topping, often infused with garlic, herbs, and a hint of Parmesan cheese. Perfect for a hearty lunch or dinner, Rigatoni with Panko can be customized with additional ingredients like roasted vegetables or chunks of sausage. The contrast between the tender pasta and the crunchy panko topping makes every bite satisfying and flavorful.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 30 minutes.
  • Preparation: 15 minutes.
  • Cooking: 15 minutes.

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) (3 minutes).
  2. Cook rigatoni: Boil rigatoni pasta according to package instructions until al dente (8 minutes).
  3. Prepare topping: Mix panko breadcrumbs with olive oil, garlic, and herbs (4 minutes).
  4. Assemble dish: Combine cooked rigatoni with your favorite sauce, place in a baking dish, and top with panko mixture (4 minutes).
  5. Bake: Place in the oven and bake until the panko is golden brown and the pasta is heated through (10 minutes).
  6. Serve: Remove from oven and let cool slightly before serving (1 minute).

Ingredients

  • Rigatoni pasta: 2 cups (200 g)
  • Panko breadcrumbs: 1 cup (100 g)
  • Olive oil: 2 tablespoons
  • Garlic: 2 cloves, minced
  • Fresh herbs: 2 tablespoons, chopped (such as basil, parsley, or thyme)
  • Parmesan cheese: 1/2 cup (50 g), grated
  • Your favorite pasta sauce: 1 cup (240 ml)
  • Salt and pepper: to taste
