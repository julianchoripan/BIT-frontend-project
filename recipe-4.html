Piadinas with Ham

Piadinas with Ham is a delightful Italian dish known for its delicious and savory flavor. This simple yet elegant meal features soft piadina flatbreads filled with thinly sliced ham, often enhanced with fresh vegetables, cheese, and a drizzle of olive oil. The dish is typically enjoyed as a quick and satisfying meal, perfect for lunch or dinner. Piadinas with Ham are versatile, allowing for a variety of fillings and toppings to suit different tastes. Their combination of tender flatbread and savory ham offers a comforting and flavorful experience in every bite.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 15 minutes.
  • Preparation: 10 minutes.
  • Cooking: 5 minutes.

Instructions

  1. Prepare ingredients: Gather all necessary ingredients, including piadina flatbreads, ham, cheese, and any desired vegetables (5 minutes).
  2. Heat piadinas: Warm piadina flatbreads in a skillet or on a grill until slightly crisp (3 minutes).
  3. Add fillings: Layer thinly sliced ham, cheese, and vegetables onto the warmed piadinas (3 minutes).
  4. Fold and cook: Fold the piadinas in half and cook for an additional 2 minutes, allowing the cheese to melt.
  5. Adjust seasoning: Taste and adjust seasoning with salt and pepper as needed (1 minute).
  6. Serve: Transfer to a serving plate and garnish with extra herbs or a drizzle of olive oil if desired (1 minute).

Ingredients

  • Piadina flatbreads: 4 pieces
  • Ham: 200 g, thinly sliced
  • Cheese: 1 cup (100 g), shredded (such as mozzarella or provolone)
  • Fresh vegetables: 1 cup (100 g), sliced (such as tomatoes, arugula, or bell peppers)
  • Olive oil: 2 tablespoons
  • Salt and pepper: to taste
  • Fresh herbs: Chopped basil or parsley to taste
