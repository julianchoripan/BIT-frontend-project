Shrimp Salad

Shrimp Salad is a refreshing and flavorful dish known for its light and zesty taste. This vibrant meal features succulent shrimp tossed with crisp, fresh vegetables and a tangy dressing, often enhanced with herbs and a hint of citrus. Perfect for a light lunch or dinner, Shrimp Salad can be customized with additional ingredients like avocado or nuts. The combination of tender shrimp and crisp greens offers a satisfying and delicious experience in every bite.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 20 minutes.
  • Preparation: 15 minutes.
  • Cooking: 5 minutes.

Instructions

  1. Prepare shrimp: Peel and devein shrimp. Cook in a skillet with a bit of olive oil until pink and opaque (5 minutes).
  2. Chop vegetables: Dice vegetables such as cucumbers, bell peppers, and cherry tomatoes (5 minutes).
  3. Mix dressing: Combine olive oil, lemon juice, salt, pepper, and any desired herbs in a small bowl (3 minutes).
  4. Combine ingredients: In a large bowl, toss cooked shrimp with chopped vegetables and dressing (5 minutes).
  5. Serve: Transfer to serving bowls or plates and garnish with extra herbs if desired (2 minutes).

Ingredients

  • Shrimp: 1 pound (450 g), peeled and deveined
  • Cucumber: 1, diced
  • Bell pepper: 1, diced
  • Lemon juice: 2 tablespoons
  • Fresh herbs: 2 tablespoons, chopped (such as parsley or basil)
  • Salt and pepper: to taste
  • Optional: Avocado, sliced or diced

Nutrition

Carbs 15 g
