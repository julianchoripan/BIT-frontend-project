Beef Lasagna

Beef Lasagna is a classic Italian dish known for its rich and hearty flavor. This comforting meal features layers of tender pasta sheets, savory ground beef cooked in a flavorful tomato sauce, and a blend of creamy cheeses, including mozzarella and ricotta. Baked until bubbling and golden, Beef Lasagna is a satisfying dish that's perfect for family dinners or special occasions. Its combination of meaty sauce and cheesy layers creates a deliciously satisfying experience with every bite.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 90 minutes.
  • Preparation: 40 minutes.
  • Cooking: 50 minutes.

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook meat: Brown ground beef with onions and garlic in a skillet; drain excess fat (10 minutes).
  3. Prepare sauce: Add tomato sauce, herbs, and seasoning to the meat; simmer for 15 minutes (15 minutes).
  4. Cook pasta: Boil lasagna noodles according to package instructions; drain (10 minutes).
  5. Assemble lasagna: Layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheese in a baking dish (10 minutes).
  6. Bake: Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes; uncover and bake for an additional 25 minutes until bubbly and golden (50 minutes).

Ingredients

  • Ground beef: 1 lb (450 g)
  • Lasagna noodles: 9-12 sheets
  • Tomato Sauce: 2 cups (480 ml)
  • Ricotta Cheese: 1 cup (240 ml)
  • Mozzarella Cheese: 2 cups (480 ml)
