Orzo with Zucchini

Orzo with Zucchini is a delightful Italian dish known for its light and fresh flavor. This simple yet elegant meal features tender orzo pasta cooked with crisp, sautéed zucchini and often enhanced with herbs and a hint of lemon. The dish is typically finished with a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese, creating a comforting and flavorful side or main course. Orzo with Zucchini is perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a healthy addition to any meal. Its combination of tender pasta and fresh vegetables offers a satisfying and refreshing taste in every bite.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 30 minutes.
  • Preparation: 10 minutes.
  • Cooking: 20 minutes.

Instructions

  1. Boil orzo: Cook orzo pasta according to package instructions (10 minutes).
  2. Prepare zucchini: Slice and sauté zucchini in olive oil until tender (8 minutes).
  3. Combine ingredients: Mix cooked orzo with sautéed zucchini, adding herbs and seasonings (5 minutes).
  4. Add cheese: Stir in grated Parmesan cheese and let it melt (2 minutes).
  5. Adjust seasoning: Taste and adjust seasoning with salt and pepper as needed (2 minutes).
  6. Serve: Transfer to a serving dish and garnish with extra herbs if desired (3 minutes).

Ingredients

  • Orzo pasta: 1 cup (200 g)
  • Zucchini: 2 medium, sliced
  • Olive oil: 2 tablespoons
  • Parmesan cheese: 1/2 cup (50 g)
  • Fresh herbs: Chopped basil to taste
  • Salt and pepper: to taste
