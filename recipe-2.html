Duck with Orange

Duck with Orange is a classic French dish known for its elegant and tangy flavor. This sophisticated meal features succulent duck breasts cooked to perfection, accompanied by a vibrant orange sauce that balances sweetness and acidity. The dish is often garnished with orange zest and served with complementary sides like roasted vegetables or potatoes. Baked until the duck skin is crispy and the sauce is bubbling, Duck with Orange is a gourmet dish perfect for special occasions or a refined dinner. Its combination of rich duck meat and zesty orange sauce creates a delightful culinary experience with every bite.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 90 minutes.
  • Preparation: 30 minutes.
  • Cooking: 60 minutes.

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Prepare duck: Score the duck breasts and season with salt and pepper (5 minutes).
  3. Sear duck: Sear the duck breasts in a hot skillet, skin-side down, until the skin is crispy and brown (10 minutes).
  4. Make sauce: In the same skillet, add orange juice, zest, and a splash of wine, cooking until slightly thickened (10 minutes).
  5. Combine: Place seared duck breasts in a baking dish, pour the orange sauce over them (5 minutes).
  6. Bake: Bake in the preheated oven until the duck is cooked to desired doneness, about 60 minutes (60 minutes).

Ingredients

  • Duck breasts: 4 pieces
  • Orange juice: 1 cup (240 ml)
  • Orange zest: 2 tablespoons
  • White wine: 1/2 cup (120 ml)
  • Salt and pepper: to taste
